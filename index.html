WELCOME TO ASTRALCOIN ECOSYSTEM.

AstralCoin (ASX) is an initiative of ZGM chess Foundation, a leading organization taking African Chess Community to it finest height.

ASX works by applying 2% transaction fee on sells and buys respectively, where 1% on every transaction is burnt forever and the remaining 1% is distributed among holders. This means that over time, ASX holders will gain a larger percentage of the total market cap. The initial total supply of ASX was 100,000,000 and is decreasing daily due to burns.

Bringing African chess scene to decentralized finance

Benefits

Trade Enhancement

ASX encourages buys and sells through enhanced innovations in the smart contract that allows certain addresses, like the Uniswap pool or exchange wallets, to be blocked from earning fess. This implementation allows 100% of the fees generated go to holders of the token. The percentage of fees earned is based on the percentage of individual holdings. This encourages buys and sells and also eradicate impatial buy high.

Fast and Reliable Payment

Unlike the centralized finance where longer days payment (est. 7 to 24 working days ) process and high charge rate cost, ASX proof to be upgraded by using decentralized finance to make prize payment to event winners within 12 to 15 blocks confirmation (est. 1 to 24 minutes ) only at the expense of gas fee. This will make it easier to register for online tournament around the globe because of AstralCoin currency pair.

Partnership and Chess Event organising

AstralCoin is developed to serve as a decentralized payment token among the chess community and other useful gaming platforms. Chess events both online and physical will be improved by Partnering with event organisers in order to improve the payment of players, discount allowance among event organisers and also for partcipation benefit of ASX holders all around the globe.
